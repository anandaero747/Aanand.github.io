About Me

I'm Apurva, a PhD student at the University of Maryland, College Park, where I am part of the Aerospace Engineering Department at the Rotorcraft Center. My research focuses on leveraging Deep Learning architectures for the design and optimization of rotorcraft and wind turbine blades. My work aims to push the boundaries of what's possible in aerodynamic efficiency and performance, contributing to the next generation of cutting-edge aerospace technology.

Research Interests

  • Neural Networks for Aerodynamics
  • Surrogate Models and Optimization
  • Wind Turbine Aerodynamics
  • Rotorcraft Aerodynamics
  • Design
  • Computational Fluid Dynamics
